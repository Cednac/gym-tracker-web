About Our App

Welcome to Gym Tracker App - your personal fitness companion. Track your workouts, monitor your progress, and achieve your fitness goals with our easy-to-use application.

Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how we handle your personal information.

Data Collection

This app stores all workout data locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit any personal information to external servers.

Data Storage

Your workout data, including exercises, sets, and personal records, are stored locally in your device's storage using SQLite.

Data Backup

You can create backups of your data which will be stored on your device. The backup feature is entirely under your control.

App Permissions

This app only requests essential permissions needed for basic functionality and local data storage.

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the app store page.
